Determination Breeds Success

This bivariate choropleth map was built in ArcMap and styled in Adobe Illustrator. The goal of this map was to show that planning to attend college after high school could have a positive efect on how well these students do in the ACT College Placement Exam. This map also takes the poverty rate as a third factor to try and parse out why these patterns appear.
