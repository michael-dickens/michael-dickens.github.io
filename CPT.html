Central Place Theory: The Rams move to L.A.

To see the effects of a potential move for St. Louis' Rams of the NFL on potential sites for relocation, I replaced the St. Louis point for one in L.A. and ran the Theissen Polygon algorithm in ArcMap. This map also had the purpose of helping to show how the real world doesn't act in a rational manner that can be predicted via methods like CPT. The map was styled and cleaned up in Adobe Illustrator.
